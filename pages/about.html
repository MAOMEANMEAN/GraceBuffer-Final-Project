Inside Gracebuffer: Our Journey,
Mission & Team

Gracebuffer was founded with a simple dream: to make bakery experiences more joyful and convenient. Instead of waiting in long lines, customers can order directly from their phones, and our team will bring the treats right to their table.

Who are we?

We are the scholarship student from ISTAD Institution of pre-university generation 5 in web design morning class.
